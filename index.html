10. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and applications include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") setting that signals your preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. Currently, there is no uniform standard for recognizing or implementing DNT signals, and we do not respond to them. If such a standard is adopted in the future and we must comply, we will update this Privacy Notice accordingly.

California law requires disclosure of how we respond to DNT signals. Since no uniform standard currently exists, we do not respond to DNT browser signals at this time.
