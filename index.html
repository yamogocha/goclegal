VEHICLE ACCIDENTS

We represent drivers and passengers injured in car accidents. to recover present and future medical expenses, lost wages (including future reduced income or lost earning potential), and compensation for pain and suffering, emotional distress, and loss of quality of life. We also work with accident reconstructionists, medical professionals, and forensic experts, if appropriate, to determine the cause of the accident and prepare your case. The firm’s personal injury practice includes car, truck, bus, taxi, rental car, bike, boat, and motorcycle accidents.

PREMISES LIABILITY

We represent people injured by unreasonably dangerous conditions on property and land. This includes injuries caused by falls from wet/slippery floors, defective steps, uneven pavement, or other dangerous walkway conditions. Other common causes of slip, trip, and fall accidents can include unsecured flooring materials, such as rugs or carpets; raised or cracked sidewalks; and sudden changes in elevation such as ramps or steps. Property owners are required to warn of hazardous conditions that exist , such as a warning that a floor had just been mopped and was still wet.

DOG ATTACKS

Our firm thoroughly investigates bites and attacks and works to hold responsible animal owners accountable under the law.

PEDESTRIAN vs. VEHICLE ACCIDENTS

California drivers are required to operate their vehicles using reasonable care. When a driver negligently strikes a pedestrian they can be held liable for the damages they cause. This may include compensation for the person’s injuries, medical bills and lost earnings or earnings capacity.

COMMERCIAL VEHICLES/ TRUCKING

Often, auto accidents involve a person driving a commercial vehicle or truck owned by another person or business. An employer can be directly liable for the negligence or wrongdoing of their employee or agent if that representative was acting on behalf of and at the direction of their boss. Employers can also be liable for negligent entrustment of the vehicle to an employee and negligent hiring of the employee.

WRONGFUL DEATH

There is no amount of compensation that will heal the loss of a family member as a result of another party’s negligence. You may be entitled to monetary damages that can help offset the financial strain you and your family may face due to your loved one’s death. We can help advocate for you and ensure your interests are protected throughout litigation against the responsible party.

CONSTRUCTION SITE ACCIDENTS

We represent individuals in claims against employers for failure to maintain safe and compliant worksites. The most common construction site accident is falling, where workers fall from high places because the ground on which workers are standing is not stable. Other common construction site accidents include electrocution, crane accidents, and defective/malfunctioning equipment.

ATTORNEYS

Greg O'Connell

Attorney & Founder

Greg O’Connell is a relentless advocate for justice. Greg combines superior trial and courtroom skills with compassionate interpersonal skills to help injured individuals and families of loved ones catastrophically injured or killed by someone else's negligence. He has more than 15 years of experience litigating high-stakes cases in the trial courts, federal courts, and courts of appeal.

Greg began his legal career as a trial attorney at the Alameda County District Attorney’s Office where he prosecuted serious felonies and obtained verdicts in 35 jury trials. During the eight years Greg served as a trial attorney in the criminal justice system, he honed courtroom-proven trial skills, poise, and knowledge of the law. Greg is not afraid to take a case to trial in order to obtain a successful outcome for a client.

After leaving the Alameda County District Attorney’s Office, Greg transitioned to plaintiff’s personal injury because he wanted to help people more directly. Greg worked for several prominent plaintiff law firms including the Dolan Law Firm and Rains Lucia Stern St. Phalle & Silver, P.C., where he made a name for himself as an aggressive and compassionate trial attorney, securing multi-million dollar settlements and verdicts for clients.

Greg has now brought his extensive knowledge and experience to GOC Legal, P.C., which he founded in 2020 with the purpose of expertly representing individuals around the Bay Area who have been injured and seek full and fair justice in the courts. He excels in all areas of litigation, from pre-trial case investigation, pre-litigation settlement offers and case strategy, to fact discovery, expert witness examination, motion practice, trial preparation, mediation, and settlement, or if necessary, trial. Greg’s courtroom skills allow him to connect with juries to achieve significant verdicts for his personal injury clients. Greg is an accomplished litigator in a variety of areas including auto accidents, auto vs. pedestrian accidents, premises liability, contract disputes, professional malpractice, trucking, and wrongful death.

Greg is a Bay Area native and resides in Oakland with his wife and dog. He is an alumnus of UCLA and earned his law degree from Golden Gate University in San Francisco. When he is not working passionately on behalf of his clients, he enjoys playing golf, swimming, spending time with his family, discovering new local restaurants, and being a proud member of Dub Nation and the 49ers Faithful.
