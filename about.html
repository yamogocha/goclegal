About GOC Legal, P.C.

Your Oakland Personal Injury Law Firm. Dedicated to Justice, Compassion, and Results

When you’ve been injured because of someone else’s negligence, you deserve more than a settlement; you deserve justice. At GOC Legal, P.C., we help individuals and families across Oakland and the greater Bay Area get the compensation and peace of mind they need to rebuild their lives.

Founded by Greg O’Connell, a former prosecutor turned personal injury attorney, GOC Legal is built on the belief that every client deserves personal attention, powerful advocacy, and unwavering support.

Our Story

GOC Legal was established with one purpose: to give injured people a voice against insurance companies and large corporations that often put profits over people.

After years as a trial attorney in the Alameda County District Attorney’s Office, Greg saw firsthand how ordinary people can be left powerless when the system fails them. That experience shaped the foundation of GOC Legal: a client-centered law firm that fights relentlessly for the rights of the injured.

Whether you’re facing mounting medical bills, emotional distress, or lost income, our team provides clear guidance, compassionate representation, and aggressive legal strategy from day one until your case is resolved.
